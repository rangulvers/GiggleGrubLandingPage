1. Introduction

Welcome to Giggle Grub! This Privacy Policy explains how our iOS application ("Giggle Grub", "the app", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our children's restaurant game.

Giggle Grub is designed with children's privacy and safety as our top priority. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and all applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

The Simple Answer: NONE

Giggle Grub does not collect any information whatsoever. Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (names, addresses, email addresses)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Contact lists or social media information
  • Gameplay statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports or performance data
  • Any other form of user data

3. How Giggle Grub Works

Giggle Grub is a completely offline application that:

  • Runs 100% locally on your device
  • Requires no internet connection to function
  • Has no backend servers or cloud services
  • Makes no API calls or network requests
  • Contains no advertising or marketing SDKs
  • Has no in-app purchases or payment processing
  • Includes no third-party analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not require any user accounts or registration

4. Data Storage

Local Storage Only

Any game preferences or settings (such as language selection or sound settings) are stored locally on your device using iOS's standard UserDefaults system. This information:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Contains no personal or identifying information

5. Third-Party Services

Giggle Grub does not integrate with or use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No backend services or APIs
  • No content delivery networks

6. Children's Privacy

Giggle Grub is designed for children and we take children's privacy extremely seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do not require any personal information to use the app
  • The app contains no social features or communication tools
  • There are no external links that could take children outside the app
  • Parents can be confident that their children's privacy is fully protected

7. Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have complete control:

  • No account creation means no information to review or delete
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data immediately
  • You can contact us anytime with questions or concerns
  • The app can be used without any privacy concerns

8. Security

Since Giggle Grub collects no data and requires no network connection:

  • There is no data to be breached or compromised
  • No personal information is at risk
  • The app operates in a completely secure, isolated environment
  • No authentication or passwords are required

9. International Use

Giggle Grub can be used anywhere in the world with the same privacy protections:

  • No data crosses international borders
  • Compliant with GDPR (European Union)
  • Compliant with COPPA (United States)
  • Compliant with PIPEDA (Canada)
  • Meets or exceeds all international privacy standards
