Giggle Grub — Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Effective: September 15, 2025

Who we are (Controller)

Malte Sohns

Short version

We don't collect, store, share, or sell any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device. No analytics. No ads. No tracking. No SDKs. No internet connection required.

1) What data do we collect?

None. We do not collect personal information, device identifiers, location, contacts, photos/videos/audio, gameplay analytics, or advertising IDs.

2) How the app works (offline by design)

  • 100% on-device; no accounts and no backend.
  • No third-party SDKs, advertising, analytics, or tracking.
  • No in-app purchases or external links for children (complies with Apple's Kids rules).

3) Local settings & TTDSG

The app stores only essential settings (e.g., sound or language) locally using iOS UserDefaults so the app functions as expected. Under Germany's TTDSG §25(2) this "strictly necessary" storage doesn't require consent. We don't set or read anything for tracking.

4) Crash diagnostics via Apple (optional)

If — and only if — you've enabled "Share with App Developers" in your device settings, Apple may provide us de-identified crash logs (e.g., OS version, stack traces). We don't combine this with anything else or try to identify you. You can turn this off at any time in Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ Analytics & Improvements. Apple's privacy policy applies to this OS-level sharing.

5) Children's privacy

Giggle Grub is built for kids and designed to avoid collecting personal data. In the EU (including Germany), children generally need parental consent for certain online data processing; because we don't process personal data, we don't ask for consent. If we ever add features that process personal data, we'll update this page first.

6) Legal bases (GDPR/BDSG)

Right now, we do not process personal data, so GDPR/BDSG legal bases don't apply. If that changes, we'll identify the relevant legal basis (e.g., Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR for features you request) and update this policy.

7) Your rights

Because we don't hold personal data about you, there's nothing to access, correct, delete, or port. If you uninstall the app, all local data is removed. You can also lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority; find German authorities via BfDI's list.

8) App Store disclosures

Our App Store listing shows "Data Not Collected." We also provide this policy via a public URL and inside the app.

9) Changes

If our practices change (for example, we add an online feature), we'll update this policy and the date at the top.
